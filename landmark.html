List of Landmarks in India

Taj Mahal

Location: Agra, Uttar Pradesh

A magnificent marble mausoleum, widely regarded as a symbol of love and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Hawa Mahal

Location: Jaipur, Rajasthan

A stunning palace known as the "Palace of Winds," featuring intricate latticework and offering panoramic views of the city.

Red Fort

Location: Delhi

A historic fort complex with impressive red sandstone architecture, serving as a symbol of India's rich history and independence.

Golden Temple

Location: Amritsar, Punjab

A revered Sikh gurdwara, known for its stunning golden exterior and serving as a place of worship and communal harmony.

Mysore Palace

Location: Mysore, Karnataka

A grand royal palace showcasing Indo-Saracenic architecture, featuring intricate interiors and illuminated during the festive season.

Gateway of India

Location: Mumbai, Maharashtra

An iconic arch monument overlooking the Arabian Sea, representing India's colonial past and serving as a popular tourist attraction.

Qutub Minar

Location: Delhi

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, a soaring minaret with intricate carvings and an important example of Indo-Islamic architecture.

Charminar

Location: Hyderabad, Telangana

A historic monument and mosque, renowned for its four grand minarets and intricate Islamic architecture, representing the rich heritage of Hyderabad.
