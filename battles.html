List of Famous Indian Battles

Battle of Plassey

Date: June 23, 1757

A decisive battle between the British East India Company and the Nawab of Bengal, Siraj ud-Daulah, resulting in a British victory and establishing their political and economic dominance in India.

Battle of Panipat

Date: January 14, 1761

A significant battle fought between the Maratha Empire led by Sadashivrao Bhau and the Durrani Empire led by Ahmad Shah Durrani, resulting in a catastrophic defeat for the Marathas and shaping the course of Indian history.

Siege of Seringapatam

Date: February 5 - May 4, 1799

A crucial siege during the Fourth Anglo-Mysore War, where the British East India Company led by General George Harris captured the fortress city of Seringapatam, resulting in the death of Tipu Sultan, the ruler of the Kingdom of Mysore.

Battle of Plassey

Date: April 13, 1919

A tragic event during the Indian independence movement, where British troops under the command of Colonel Reginald Dyer opened fire on a peaceful gathering at Jallianwala Bagh in Amritsar, Punjab, leading to hundreds of deaths and widespread condemnation.

Battle of Longewala

Date: December 4-7, 1971

A significant battle fought between the Indian Army and the Pakistan Army during the Indo-Pakistani War of 1971, where a small Indian infantry company successfully defended the Longewala border post against a large Pakistani assault, inflicting heavy casualties on the enemy.
